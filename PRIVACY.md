# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 17 June 2026
**Service:** GENOMA Labs inference API ("the Service"), including the KALYPSO model family.
**Provider:** GENOMA Labs ("we", "us").

GENOMA Labs operates an API that serves open-weight AI models. This policy
explains what we process when you send requests to the Service, including
through routing partners such as OpenRouter.

## 1. What we process

- **API requests and responses.** The prompts you submit and the completions we
  return are processed to generate and deliver model output.
- **Account and billing data.** Identifiers, API keys, and usage counts needed
  to authenticate requests, meter usage, and bill or pay through our routing
  partners.
- **Technical metadata.** Timestamps, token counts, latency, model ID, error
  codes, and IP address, used to operate, secure, and debug the Service.

## 2. How we use it

- To provide model inference and return results.
- To meter usage, calculate charges, and reconcile payments with routing partners.
- To monitor reliability and performance, prevent abuse, and investigate
  security incidents.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Training

We do **not** use your prompts or completions to train or fine-tune our models
without your explicit consent. Request content is processed only to serve your
request and for the operational purposes above.

## 4. Retention

We retain request content only as long as needed to deliver the response and
support short-term reliability and abuse monitoring, then delete or aggregate it.
Billing and account records are retained as required for accounting and legal
purposes.

## 5. Sharing

We share data only with:
- **Routing partners** (e.g., OpenRouter) that broker requests to the Service,
  limited to what is needed to route and bill.
- **Infrastructure providers** that host our inference compute, under
  confidentiality obligations.
- **Authorities**, where required by law.

We do not sell personal data.

## 6. Security

We use access controls, encrypted transport (HTTPS), and least-privilege
handling of keys and request data. No system is perfectly secure; we work to
protect data in line with industry practice.

## 7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access to, correction of, or
deletion of your personal data. Contact us at the address below.

## 8. International processing

The Service may process data on infrastructure located in multiple regions.
By using the Service you understand requests may be processed outside your country.

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by a new effective
date on this page.
